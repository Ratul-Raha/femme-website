# Femme Beauty Clinic — Design System

## Brand Vision
Femme Beauty Clinic should feel like a premium traditional beauty parlour with elegance, confidence, and femininity. The atmosphere should combine **royal South Asian bridal luxury** with a modern clean beauty-clinic experience. It should appeal strongly to Bangladeshi women, especially audiences in Sylhet.

**Core Mood Words:** Sassy, Royal, Feminine, Luxurious, Trustworthy, Bridal, Classical, Warm.

---

## Audience
- Women in Bangladesh (primary)
- Brides-to-be
- Beauty-conscious professionals
- Women seeking skincare / salon / makeover services
- Sylhet local premium clientele

---

## Regional Identity (Sylhet Vibe)
Subtle inspiration from Sylhet:
- Tea garden freshness
- Elegant green landscape contrast accents
- Refined urban premium taste
- Family-trusted parlour culture
- Wedding & celebration demand

Use subtle photography and textures inspired by:
- Soft floral embroidery
- Traditional ornaments
- Bengali bridal detailing
- Premium salon interiors
